About Me

I am a Computer Science student at Cebu Institute of Technology – University with a strong passion for technology and problem-solving.

My interest in this field started when I was in senior high school, where I found myself unsure about which path to take. While reflecting on my interests, I remembered how I used to create games on Roblox — an experience that sparked my curiosity for programming and building interactive systems.

That realization led me to pursue Computer Science, where I can turn my creativity and logical thinking into practical skills. Currently, I am focused on improving my knowledge in programming and software development, while continuously exploring new technologies and areas within the tech industry.

I enjoy learning by doing — whether it's through academic projects or personal experiments — and I am always looking for ways to grow as a developer. My goal is to build meaningful and efficient solutions while continuously improving my skills and understanding of technology.
